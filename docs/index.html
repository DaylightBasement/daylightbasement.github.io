Progression of Colors

 Posted on June 17, 2020  |  dbstudio

I find it hard to consider myself an artist. I’ve always been drawn to coding and building before what I consider traditional art. I’ve always had an interest in art, but never thought I was any good. I find, generally, that my art practices mimic my coding practices: I often start with abstract ideas then slowly progress to a well defined final product.
